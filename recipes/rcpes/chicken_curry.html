Chicken Curry

Description

A flavorful chicken curry rich with spices and tender chicken pieces, simmered in a creamy sauce. This dish is a staple in various cuisines and can be served with rice or naan bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tsp ginger paste
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into chunks
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan and cook the onion until soft. Add the garlic and ginger, cooking for another minute.
  2. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, cumin, chili powder, and paprika, and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and brown them on all sides.
  4. Pour in the diced tomatoes and coconut milk, and bring to a simmer. Cook for 20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Season with salt to taste and garnish with chopped cilantro before serving.
